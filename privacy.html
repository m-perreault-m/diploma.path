Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Diploma Path is a free planning tool. We do not ask you to create an account and we do not sell personal information.

The site uses Google AdSense to display ads. AdSense may use cookies or similar technologies to show relevant ads and measure performance. You can manage ad personalization in your Google ad settings.

If you choose to save a pathway, it is stored locally in your browser so you can return to it later. You can clear saved data anytime using the “Start a new pathway” button in the app.
